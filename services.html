Our Services

Customizable Accessories

Pet Plus offers a wide range of customizable accessories designed to make your pet stand out and feel special. Our collection includes personalized collars, engraved tags, and custom-fit harnesses, allowing you to add a unique touch to every item. Choose from various colors, patterns, and materials to create accessories that perfectly match your pet’s personality. We also offer a selection of bespoke pet beds and blankets that can be customized with your pet’s name or a favorite design. Each product is crafted with attention to detail and high-quality materials, ensuring both comfort and durability. Our commitment to personalization means that every accessory is made to order, giving your pet a truly one-of-a-kind experience. Explore our customizable options and provide your pet with accessories that are as unique as they are lovable.

Subscription Boxes

Our subscription boxes are the ultimate convenience for pet owners looking to keep their pets happy. Each month, our expertly curated boxes deliver a delightful assortment of toys, treats, and essentials right to your doorstep. We carefully select high-quality products that cater to your pet’s needs and preferences, ensuring every box is packed with excitement and variety. Our subscription service is fully customizable—simply choose your pet’s size, dietary restrictions, and any specific preferences, and we’ll tailor each box accordingly. Whether your pet loves chew toys, interactive games, or gourmet treats, our subscription boxes provide a continuous supply of enjoyment and enrichment. Enjoy the convenience of doorstep delivery and the joy of discovering new favorites for your pet each month. With our subscription service, keeping your pet entertained and well-cared-for has never been easier.

Virtual Trainings

We’re excited to offer virtual training services designed to help you and your pet thrive together. Our expert trainers provide personalized guidance through convenient online sessions, allowing you to address behavior issues, learn new commands, or enhance your pet’s skills from the comfort of your home. Our virtual training covers a range of topics, from basic obedience and puppy training to more advanced techniques and behavioral modifications. Each session is tailored to your pet’s specific needs and learning style, ensuring effective and engaging training experiences. With flexible scheduling and one-on-one attention, our virtual training services fit seamlessly into your busy life. Whether you’re a first-time pet owner or looking to refine your pet’s existing skills, our experienced trainers are here to support you every step of the way. Empower yourself with the tools and knowledge to build a strong, positive relationship with your pet.
